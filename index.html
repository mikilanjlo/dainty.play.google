Unlocker Puzzle

DAINTY Puzzle
Contains Ads

Spin the wheel, calculate a few moves ahead and win! Four unique modes and many levels will make your brain work at its maximum.🤯 Train your tactical thinking and memory, keep your mind active!

The rules are simple:
- The ball moves left and right, bouncing off the inside of the wheel
- When a ball and a segment collide, their values ​​are summed up
- Spin the wheel so that the ball hits the desired segment
- Receive the required amount the specified number of times to win.

Game modes:
- Numerical. It's time to do some calculations. This is where your wits and some arithmetic come in handy.
- Color. Color mode will give you a palette of colors. Mix colors like a real artist!🌈
- Figured. It's time to remember geometry! In figure mode, you will have to connect dots and build shapes.🔺

Play and compete with your friends! Which one of you will be able to complete more levels?🤔
